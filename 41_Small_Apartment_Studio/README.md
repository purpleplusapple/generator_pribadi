# Small Apartment Studio AI

Premium AI-powered studio apartment design & makeover application.

## Features
- **AI Redesign**: Transform your small studio into a spacious, zoned home.
- **Smart Zoning**: Optimize sleep, work, and living areas.
- **Style Selection**: Choose from Scandi Compact, Japandi Calm, and more.
- **Compare**: Tap to see before/after results.
- **History**: Save and organize your designs.
